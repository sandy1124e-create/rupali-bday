Humare bich bohot saare jhagde hua hai chahe wo meri galti ho ya fir koi saap, dyne aag laga ke gye ho,

Par har baar kaise pata nhi hum zyada ache dost bante gye,

Kabhi kabhi mai bhi srsly ghusse mei aa jata hu toh kaise pata nhi aap manane aa jate ho, Kabhi bhagwan se aapke liye dua mangta hu toh aap maan jaate ho.

Toh ab mujhe iss baat ka dar nhi ki humara jhagda hoga ki nhi

Hoga bhi toh ghumke aa hi jaoge aapne bola hi hai aur mai toh hu hi manane ke liye.
