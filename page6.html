Kehne ko toh ye aapka bday hai aapko gift dena chahiye par,

Aapka meri life mei aana mere liye duniya ka sabse bada gift hai,

Aap sachme bohot hi ache ho.

Aisa nhi hai ki is se pehle mera koi bestfriend nhi tha.

Aap ko pata hi hai ek saap ko pal le bitha ke rakha tha

Mujhe wo rulati depression baaki ke kahaniya toh aapko pata hi hai.
